Description

Towards A Good Life

“Where all that you wish for is already granted.”

Bungalows are of an identifiable house form. Even as Triplexes or Quadruplexes, they stressed their single house precedence. Refined by traditional architectural elements, they spoke to both a house that nurtured families and to a street that gathered them into a Neighborhood. They symbolized a home setting and civic culture that are true to the core of “ALAKNANDA”.

Alaknanda with its airstocratic ambience, the majestic entrance gate, vast open spaces with exceptional features and facilities is truly unique in its conception.

The project has been conceived painstakingly to offer hightened comfort. The layout of the project offers openness to all bungalows, intelligent interior planning of the bungalows maximizes comfort and utility.

Alaknanda is strategically located in the heart of Navsari. These bungalows are complete with every modern amenities and an infrastructure that is guaranteed to give you a world-class living experience.

Alaknanda will be more than your home. It will be your rejuventation therapy, where you'll wash away the stress and fatigue of a working day. As we said, here you will get a complete world, full of surprises. All will be equipped with the finest and most luxurious amenities.

For those of you who wish to have a private house structure with the land ownership rights, Bungalows serve the best option. Bungalows are of an identifiable house form. The precedence of a single house has always been stressed from the time in history when there were only individual houses and not any form of high rises. And till date many prefer to live the grandeur life by owing a land and calling it their own. Alaknanda offers the high strata population a choice to own a 3 / 4 BHK Bungalow in a posh locality with an option to get it designed by our onboard Professional Architects and Interior Designers. So, apart from getting a customized luxurious abode, one can even select the area spread that an individual family wants, unlike the fixed area system offered by others in the business. The premises include a Club House, Gymnasium, an Open Party Plot, and a Common Plot which can be utilized as Children Play Area, Garden or Senior Citizen Sitting Area. The project has a total of 70 plots out of which 53 are already booked and sold.
