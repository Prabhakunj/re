About Us


Prabhakunj Realty is the sister concern of Prabhakunj Group. Prabhakunj Group started off its journey with the Realty Sector under the name Prabhakunj Group during the year 2011.

We have completed 9 huge projects since our inception, catering different sections of the society. Prabhakunj Realty caters to all the different sections of society i.e. Luxurious Premium, Upmiddle, Middle & Lower section of society.

Majorly we are indulge in Affordable Housing Projects, as they form around 70% to 80% of our society. After conduction a survey we came to know the requirements & needs of the common man.

Since its inception, Prabhakunj Realty is constantly analysing the customers need of all segments and utilizing the some while executing projects. That is why Prabhakunj Realty takes care of simplifying financial concerns of customers and has initiated a scheme, wherein the customers get all the facilities for a truly balance life.

Along with this Prabhakunj also envision to offer quality residence at the most affordable price and hense it has become a widely appreciated name for residential & commercial in Navsari.

With our initial projects we realized that a common man, after fulfilling his long cherished dream of having his own Home, doesn't has any money or savings left with him. And, a lot more than the four Walls and a Roof, is needed to covert a house into a home. Also, people earning everyday for their living, do not have the time to run around managing to get an Electric connection or a LPG Connection, or even running to the different Banks to get their Home Loans sanctioned or go to the Federal Authorities for Documentation procedure. After giving it a lot of thought, and realizing that our end goal is still a few hurdles away, we finally came up with the idea of hiring a team of professionals to do all the subsidiary work of buying a new house. We offered this special service to our customers with no extra charge.

Over the years, we have grown and gained experience and build connections with many providers and sources, just so we can provide our client the best with the worth of every penny he pays.

We provide end to end services from Registration, Documentation, Legal Fomalities, Bank Formalities of getting the Loan Sanctioned, to Water Supply, Electric Supply and Cooking Gas connection to fully furnished & Interior designed by our professionals to the ultimate goal of providing a dream house.

Many of our projects are architecturally designed according to the norms for availing the benefits of Subsidy under the Pradhan Mantri Avas Yojna, where the customer gets Rs.2,67,000/- subsidy from the loan amount to be paid. Also some of our projects have Move-In option, wherein you are suppose to walk into a fully furnished abode designed by our Proffesional Interior Designers.

We have 2 Upcoming Projects, the details of which will be announced soon.

So, All We Do Is Provide A House For You To Make It A Home!
