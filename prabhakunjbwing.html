Description

After gaining some experience and learning the basics of the Realty Sector, our next project made us move out of the customized market and enter into the market of the Common Man who just wanted a living space which he can call his own. This project was completed in December 2014. Prabhakunj B is a 5-storey Apartment Structure comprising of 44Dwelling Units, a Common Terrace and a Ground Level Parking. Each of these individual units have a 1 / 2 BHK concept with a private balcony; spread on 600 sq. ft. to 925 sq. ft. of Super build-up area. Current occupancy of this project is 100%. And yes, Prabhakunj B was initiated prior to Prabhakunj A.
