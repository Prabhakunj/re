Description

Prabhakunj Jil is the most recently completed project of Prabhakunj Realty, the completion period be marked at the end of August' 2018. This was our first Residential-Cum-Commercial Project, comprising of 35 Dwelling Units of varied areas and 8 Commercial Units. The structure of Prabhakunj Jil has its base level as the Commercial Setup and from the 1stto the 5thstorey, the Residential Units take their shape. Except for 1 Commercial Unit, every unit of this Project is sold out. The target population of Prabhakunj Jil was the Middle Class section of the society whose earning capacity even though limited, do had a wish for their own house. This project avails the benefits of subsidy amounting to Rs.2,67,000/- under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. And we provided a fully furnished accommodation which had all basic amenities to all the customers.
