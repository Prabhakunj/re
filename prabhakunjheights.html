Description

Home Is Where Your Story Begins

As the name suggests, Prabhakunj Heights is the towering structure of the town. This is a 10-storey Residential-Cum-Commercial Project comprising of 50 luxurious Dwelling Spaces and 25 Commercial Units. This Project caters to the needs of higher strata of the population, who want a royal lifestyle with all modern amenities and luxuries. The units are lavishly designed by our onboard Professional Interior Designers and the Architectural Planning is done keeping in mind the provision for proper ventilation and optimum natural light. We offer 2 / 3 / 4 BHK units with different measuring areas ranging from 1,200sq.ft. to 2,300sq. ft. Out of the total Commercial blocks, only 2 units remain unsold and the Residential Block statistics state that more than 70% of the units are sold out. This is an ongoing project and 80% of the units are already booked.
